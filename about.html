About AeroNext

We are a team of passionate engineers, environmental scientists, and AI experts dedicated to transforming urban air quality through innovative technology solutions.

Since our founding in 2018, we've been on a mission to make clean air accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission & Vision

At AeroNext, we believe that clean air is a fundamental human right. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge air purification systems that are intelligent, efficient, and accessible.

We leverage the latest advancements in AI, IoT, and data analytics to create systems that don't just filter air, but understand and adapt to their environment.

Our vision is a world where urban environments are no longer associated with poor air quality, where everyone can breathe easily regardless of where they live.
